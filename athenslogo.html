Tourism Logo

Creating a logo for Athens, Greece

We had to design a logo for an international city that accurately represented their history, culture, and arts.

I chose Athens, Greece for this project because I thought it would be fun and interesting to learn about their long history that traced back to Ancient Greece; not to mention the city being named after Athena, the greek goddess of wisdom and war.
